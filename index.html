Dog Age & Zodiac Converter

Welcome to the Dog Age & Zodiac Converter. This tool helps you convert your dog's age into human years and reveals their position in the dog zodiac cycle. Our converter takes into account the unique pattern of dog years, where each year is usually 52 days long, except for the last year of the cycle, which is 53 days. In leap years, this extends to 54 days.
