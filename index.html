Dog Age & Zodiac Converter

This tool converts a dog's age to its equivalent in human years and provides its position in the dog zodiac cycle. The dog zodiac cycle consists of seven years, each corresponding to a specific letter and number in the sequence. In this system, a typical year is divided into seven dog years, each consisting of 52 days, except the last dog year, which has 53 days. During leap years, the last dog year extends to 54 days, aligning with the additional day in the human calendar.
