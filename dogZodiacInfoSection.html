Dog Celestial Signs

Explore the unique pattern of dog years, where each year is usually 52 days long, except for the last year of the cycle, which is 53 days. On leap years, this extends to 54 days.

  • The Adventurer Jan 1 - Feb 21

    Energetic and curious, always ready for a new challenge or exploration.

  • The Braveheart Feb 22 - Apr 14

    Courageous and loyal, often acting as protectors and devoted companions.

  • The Companion Apr 15 - Jun 5

    Companion dogs excel in empathy and affection, providing comfort and friendship like no other.

  • The Dreamer Jun 6 - Jul 28

    Thoughtful and introspective, often seen gazing into the distance or enjoying peaceful moments.

  • The Explorer Jul 29 - Sep 19

    Full of curiosity and a sense of adventure, always eager to discover new places and smells.

  • The Faithful Sep 20 - Nov 10

    The epitome of loyalty, offering unwavering support and companionship to their loved ones.

  • The Guardian Nov 11 - Dec 31

    Natural protectors, known for their alertness and strong sense of duty towards their families.
